Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about QuickLoan India.

What documents are required for an instant loan? +

You require an identity proof (Aadhaar or PAN), address proof, and income proof like a recent payslip or 3-month bank statement.

How soon is the amount disbursed? +

After your documents are validated and approved, the funds are deposited directly into your bank account within 24 hours.

You can apply for amounts ranging from a minimum of ₹10,000 to a maximum of ₹5,00,000.
